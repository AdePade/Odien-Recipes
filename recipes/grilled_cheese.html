Grilled Cheese

Description

One of Americas favoirte snacks. Grilled cheses has lunch covered. Easy and deliecious!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat Pan
  2. Coat with butter
  3. Place two slices of bread in pan
  4. Add Cheese
  5. Cook until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown
  6. Let cool
  7. Slice
