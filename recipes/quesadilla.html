Quesadilla

Description

Quesadilla's are a never miss quick meal that everyone is sure to enjoy! Feed everyone with this Mexican classic meal!

Ingredients

  • Tortillia
  • Cheese
  • Chicken
  • Bell Peppers

Steps

  1. Heat and oil pan on medium heat
  2. Put torillia in pan
  3. Cover with cheese
  4. Put on toppings
  5. Fold
  6. Take off heat
  7. Cool
  8. Slice
